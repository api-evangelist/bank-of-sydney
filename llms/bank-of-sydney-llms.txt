# Bank of Sydney

> Bank of Sydney Ltd is an Australian authorised deposit-taking institution (ADI),
> a wholly owned subsidiary of Bank of Beirut, offering personal and business banking.
> As a Consumer Data Right (CDR) data holder it exposes a public, unauthenticated
> Product Reference Data (PRD) API conforming to the DSB Consumer Data Standards.
> Consumer account/transaction data is available only to Accredited Data Recipients
> (ADRs) under CDR authorisation (OAuth2/OIDC, FAPI-hardened, mutual-TLS).
